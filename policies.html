Our Legal Policies

Privacy Policy

At TripNest, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our travelers. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to ensure your personal data is handled appropriately.

Information Collection and Use

  • We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you book a trip or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • This information is exclusively used to process your bookings, communicate travel itineraries, and improve the quality of our services.
  • We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third-party marketing agencies. Necessary information may be shared with trusted local operators and accommodations strictly for the purpose of facilitating your trip.

Data Security

TripNest employs industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive information. We use encrypted payment gateways and secure server architecture to ensure your personal and financial details remain completely confidential throughout your transaction.

Terms and Conditions

By accessing or using the TripNest website and booking our services, you agree to be bound by the following Terms and Conditions.

Booking & Services

All bookings are subject to availability. To secure your reservation, an advance payment is required as outlined during the checkout process. The itinerary detailed on our website is an approximate schedule; TripNest reserves the right to modify routes or accommodations due to unforeseen circumstances such as severe weather, road blockages, or other natural anomalies inherent to Himalayan travel.

Participant Responsibility

Travelers are responsible for assessing their physical fitness before participating in challenging treks or high-altitude journeys. TripNest holds no liability for injuries, health issues, or loss of personal belongings during the trip. We strictly advise obtaining adequate travel and medical insurance prior to your departure.

Code of Conduct

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy against disrespect toward local cultures, ecosystems, or fellow travelers. TripNest reserves the right to expel any individual from a tour without refund if their behavior is deemed hazardous or deeply offensive to the group or environment.

Refund Policy

We understand that sometimes travel plans must change. Our refund policy has been structured to balance flexibility for our travelers with the logistical commitments we make to our local partners.

Advance Payment Refund Rule

We will issue a 30% refund of the advance amount paid by the client, provided the cancellation claim is officially made 4 to 5 days before the scheduled trip departure date.

  • Past the Due Date: If a cancellation is requested less than 4 days before the travel date, or on/after the departure date, we will unfortunately not be able to issue any refund. This strict policy is due to the non-refundable nature of the immediate bookings, permits, and transportation reservations made on your behalf as the trip date approaches.
  • Processing Time: Eligible refunds will be processed back to the original method of payment within 7 to 10 business days after the cancellation has been approved by our team.
  • Trip Modifications: If you wish to simply reschedule your trip instead of canceling entirely, please contact our support team at least 7 days prior to departure. Rescheduling is subject to availability and may incur nominal administrative or seasonal difference charges.
