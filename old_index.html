Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Delhi → Guptkashi

Board overnight journey from Akshardham Metro Station, Delhi towards Guptkashi.

2

Devprayag → Guptkashi

Visit Devprayag where the Bhagirathi and Alaknanda rivers merge to form the sacred Ganga. Continue to Guptkashi for overnight stay.

3

Trek to Kedarnath

Travel to Sonprayag then Gaurikund. Begin the sacred trek to Kedarnath and attend the divine evening Aarti ceremony.

4

Kedarnath Sightseeing

Visit Kedarnath Temple, Bhairavnath Temple, Bhim Sheela, and Adi Shankaracharya Cave. Trek back to Guptkashi.

5

Journey to Badrinath

Travel to Badrinath or Pandukeshwar through stunning mountain passes. Check in for overnight stay.

6

Badrinath & Mana Village

Visit Badrinath Temple, Tapt Kund, and Mana Village — Ganesh Gufa, Vyasa Gufa, Bhim Pul, Saraswati River, and the Last Tea Shop of India. Begin return to Delhi.

7

Arrive Delhi

Reach Delhi early morning with divine blessings and lifelong memories of the Himalayas.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Delhi → Haridwar

Pickup from Delhi Airport / Railway Station / Home. Drive to Haridwar (220 km / 6-7 hrs). Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Dinner & overnight stay in Haridwar.

2

Haridwar → Barkot

Drive Haridwar → Barkot. Distance 180 km / 7-8 hrs. Enjoy beautiful Himalayan views. Dinner & overnight stay in Barkot.

3

Barkot → Yamunotri → Barkot

Drive Barkot → Janki Chatti (45 km). Trek 6 km to Yamunotri Temple. Temple darshan & pooja. Return trek and drive back to Barkot. Overnight stay.

4

Barkot → Uttarkashi

Drive Barkot → Uttarkashi (100 km). Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple. Dinner & overnight stay.

5

Uttarkashi → Gangotri → Uttarkashi

Drive Uttarkashi → Gangotri (100 km). Holy dip in Bhagirathi River. Gangotri Temple Darshan. Return to Uttarkashi.

6

Uttarkashi → Guptkashi

Drive Uttarkashi → Guptkashi. Distance 220 km / 8-9 hrs. Dinner & overnight stay.

7

Guptkashi → Kedarnath

Drive Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund. Trek 16-18 km to Kedarnath Temple. Options: Pony / Doli / Helicopter. Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

8

Kedarnath → Guptkashi

Morning Kedarnath Darshan. Trek down to Gaurikund. Drive to Guptkashi. Overnight stay.

9

Guptkashi → Badrinath

Drive Guptkashi → Badrinath (200 km). Visit Tapt Kund. Badrinath Temple Darshan. Overnight stay.

10

Badrinath → Rudraprayag

Visit Mana Village (Last Indian Village). Drive Badrinath → Rudraprayag. Overnight stay.

11

Rudraprayag → Delhi

Drive Rudraprayag → Delhi (400 km). Drop at Airport / Railway Station / Home.

Badrinath → Delhi
